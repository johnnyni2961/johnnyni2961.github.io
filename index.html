Hackathons

Pinnacle. As an organizer of Pinnacle, amongst tens of other hackathons, I've gained the insight of what it takes to manage sponsorships, marketing, as well as collaborating with a diverse team. However, Pinnacle is no ordinary hackathon, its The Olympics of Hackathons. Only the top 200 qualifying hackers from across the world compete in Pinnacle, however, there's a twist. Hackers can only qualify for Pinnacle if they are ranked as the winner in any partnering, prestigious hackathons, such as: HackDuke, HackUMass, HackDavis, CruzHacks, Cal Hacks, BostonHacks, Hack Dartmouth, HackPrinceton, HackNYU, HackGT, IvyHacks, UofTHacks, and more!
